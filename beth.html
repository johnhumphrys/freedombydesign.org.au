Beth is a very special taxi

Beth is a Social Enterprise Taxi that has just started her 5th year of service in the Yarra Valley, Victoria, Australia.

Based in Healesville, Beth does her best to operate as a reliable, safe transport service and has recently become an independent taxi.

Bookings

We encourage booking trips in advance so we can help people to arrive at their appointments and destinations on time.

Beth stays local to Healesville on weekend nights. We have a number of options to help people who need to travel further. During the week, Beth does longer trips to help community members get to important medical appointments.

To advance book Beth, please phone and speak with her driver on 0499 773 393. Beth is currently working limited hours, so leave a message if the phone is unattended.

We also take bookings in advance by email fbdvic@gmail.com and through Beth's Facebook page
