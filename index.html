A quick update,

Hello,

This was a very difficult decision for us to make. Many Healesville community members and businesses rely on Beth in their daily life.

We really appreciate everyone's support in helping us build our Social Enterprise and will continue to work hard to figure out how to safely provide this essential local taxi service.

Beth the Social Enterprise is off the road~!!!

Everyone who loves Beth knows that our main aim is to help keep people safe. Beth is known for her use of eucalyptus spray... it smells great; but more importantly, it helps keep people safe from airborne viruses.

We have done our research and can't guarantee that our eucalyptus spray or any other available products will protect people from being exposed to Corona Virus while they travel in Beth.

We think it is safer for our drivers, passengers and the broader community if we park Beth. The status of the COVID-19 situation will be closely monitored we will review our decision on a weekly basis.

Stay safe and travel well.







Who are we?

Freedom by Design Inc. is an emerging organisation that is passionate about inclusive living. We step away from a welfare mindset and build partnerships with community groups and organisations, social enterprises, businesses and corporations to help us build a sustainable community organisation.

We are proudly open, honest and transparent in our business and service provision. We do not do things just because we need to be compliant with disability standards. We do things because we believe in them.
