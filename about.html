Our Vision:

All people, without exception, have talents and dreams; and can live valuable, rich lives of their own choosing with the right support

Our Mission:

We walk through life with those we serve as they turn their dreams into reality and build great lives

  • We will do this by deeply listening and enabling people to make decisions about their lives in partnership with those they love and trust
  • We help people live life their own way; by providing customized and personalised supports, promoting community membership, and sustainability

Our Values

  • We embrace diversity
  • All people are equal; all people need support from others in their life
  • All people can live in their own home with the right support
  • Every person contributes to their community
  • When people are included, valued and respected, they become valued members of their community
  • When people connect with likeminded people, amazing things happen
  • Healthy relationships are the essence our lives and the work we do
  • Respect and trust are paramount; nothing about a person without the person
  • All people learn as they live; the best learning occurs in natural settings
  • We challenge assumptions that lead to a person being segregated and isolated
